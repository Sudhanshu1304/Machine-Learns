Using Autoencoder

Previous Next

Autoencoder is an unsupervised Learning approach. It is an Artificial Neural Network(ANN) that learns to capture important piece of information (features) from a given data . AN Autoencoder forms a representation of the actuall data into a samll maxtrx also known as latent sapce.

Autoencoder have four major components:
  • Encoder - It compreses (encodes) the data into a latent space (bottleneck).
  • Bottleneck - Is the layer that contains the compressed representation of the input data. This is the lowest possible dimensions of data.
  • Decoder - This part of the model learns to reconstruct the input from the latent space (latent representation).
  • Reconstruction Loss - This is a method that determines how close is the output is to the original input.
