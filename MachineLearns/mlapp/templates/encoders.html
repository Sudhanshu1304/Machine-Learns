Autoencoder is an unsupervised Learning approach. It is an Artificial Neural Network(ANN) that learns how to capture the most important piece of information and hence helps in removing noise and reducing dimensions.
Autoencoder have four major components:
  • Encoder - This part of the model learns to encode the original data into a lower latent space(lower dimensions) by focusing on the most important pieces of the data.
  • Bottle Neck - which is the layer that contains the compressed representation of the input data. This is the lowest possible dimensions of the input data.
  • Decoder - In which the model learns how to reconstruct the data from the encoded representation to be as close to the original input as possible.
  • Reconstruction Loss: - This is a method that determines how close is the output is to the original input.
They can be used as a generative Model or for dimensionality reduction. So basically
encoder acts as a recognition network.
and the decoder as a generative model.
